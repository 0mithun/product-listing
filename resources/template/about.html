Hummingbird Art and Design was founded in 2014 by Bunnie Wissman, visionary art and antiques gallerist.  She is known by both her clients and peers for her unparalleled talent in sourcing the finest modern and contemporary art and rare antiques.  After many years in the trade, Bunnie is adding a new facet to her design and interior decoration services by offering her own curated collection of beautiful and unique art objects. These objects have been sourced from highly skilled and imaginative artists and artisans from a wide variety of traditions and places.

We hope that you enhance your own interior spaces with the handmade artistry and high design of these fascinating pieces.
